Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ZAP Analytics ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects information in connection with the ZAP Peptides & TRT Tracker application and website at zaptracker.ca (collectively, the "Service").

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

What Information Do We Collect?

Information you provide directly

  • Email address — collected when you create an account via Firebase Authentication.
  • Protocol data — doses, compounds, injection logs, blood work results, body composition entries, and personal records that you enter into the app. This data is stored in your account on Firebase Firestore.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and interactions with the app, collected via Google Analytics (GA4) to help us understand how the Service is used.
  • Device information — device type, operating system version, and browser type, collected automatically by Firebase and Google Analytics.
  • IP address — collected by Firebase and Google Analytics as part of standard web and app traffic logging.

How Do We Use Your Information?

  • To provide and maintain the Service, including syncing your protocol data across devices when you are signed in.
  • To authenticate your identity via Firebase Authentication.
  • To store and retrieve your protocol data via Firebase Firestore.
  • To understand how users interact with the Service so we can improve it (Google Analytics).
  • To communicate with you about your account or the Service when necessary.

Do We Sell Your Data?

No. We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information or protocol data to third parties for commercial purposes. Your health and protocol data is yours.

Third-Party Services We Use

Firebase (Google LLC)

We use Firebase services provided by Google LLC, including:

  • Firebase Authentication — manages account creation and sign-in using your email address and password.
  • Cloud Firestore — stores your protocol data (doses, compounds, blood work, body composition, personal records) in Google's cloud infrastructure.

Firebase is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

Google Analytics (GA4)

We use Google Analytics 4 to collect anonymized usage data about how the Service is used. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Analytics is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

How Is Your Data Stored and Protected?

Your protocol data is stored in Firebase Firestore, which is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure with industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. Access to your data is protected by Firebase Authentication — only you can access your account data.

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.

Data Retention

We retain your account and protocol data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be deleted from our systems within 30 days. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us (see below).

Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you.
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Portability — request an export of your protocol data.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to know what personal information we collect, request deletion of your personal information, and opt out of the sale of personal information. We do not sell personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
